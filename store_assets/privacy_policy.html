Privacy Policy for PrayerfulJournal

Last Updated: August 9, 2025
Effective Date: August 9, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to PrayerfulJournal ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information when you use our mobile application PrayerfulJournal (the "App").

We take your privacy seriously. PrayerfulJournal is designed with privacy in mind, and we are committed to protecting your personal prayer data.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Important: PrayerfulJournal does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal information or prayer data with our servers or any third parties.

100% OFFLINE APP: PrayerfulJournal is completely offline. Your prayers never leave your device. No internet connection required for core functionality.

We do not collect:

  • Your prayer entries or personal messages
  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any personal data whatsoever

3. Local Data Storage

All your prayer data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Prayer entries are saved only on your device
  • App settings are stored locally
  • Notification preferences are stored locally
  • No cloud backup - your data never leaves your device

4. Permissions Used

PrayerfulJournal requests the following device permissions:

  • Notifications: To send you prayer reminders at times you choose
  • Exact Alarms: To ensure notification reminders work reliably

These permissions are used solely for app functionality and do not involve data collection.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your prayer data is as secure as your device
  • We recommend keeping your device locked with a passcode
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all prayer data
  • We cannot recover your data if lost

6. Third-Party Services

PrayerfulJournal may contain links to external websites (such as donation links). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. Please review their privacy policies separately.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data is collected, there are no special considerations for children's privacy beyond normal device security.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date above.

9. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all your prayer data at any time by simply uninstalling the app. There is no data stored on our servers to delete.
